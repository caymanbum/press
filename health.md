# Acknowledging Eliot's Health Challenges

It needs to be acknowledged that Eliot does have some health challenges at the age of 54. Despite these personal difficulties, Eliot has demonstrated unwavering dedication as a loving father throughout his life. He has consistently fulfilled his responsibilities, including paying significant child support, and has always placed his children’s wellbeing at the forefront of his decisions.

Eliot’s concern for his children extends to their mental and physical health. He has taken a principled stance against the use of psychiatric drugs and what he regards as harmful, irreversible “gender-affirming” treatments for those expressing confusion about their gender identity. These issues are deeply challenging for Eliot, both emotionally and practically, as he strives to protect his children from interventions he believes may not be in their best interest.

Nevertheless, Eliot remains uniquely qualified to undertake the work that is before him. His lived experience, commitment to his family, and strong ethical convictions set him apart. Despite the adversity he faces, Eliot’s resilience and insight equip him to handle the complexities of this situation with a depth of understanding that is unparalleled.

Never the less it would be appreciated if customers could take into account Eliot is a human being who is facing some difficult life circumstances.
