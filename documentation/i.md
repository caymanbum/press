# Documentation Unleashed!

We've got access to the WordPress site, where we can find the amazing documentation for Iguana 5 and 6 - check it out here: [https://help.interfaceware.com/](https://help.interfaceware.com/)

Moving forward, our docs will be written in a system that's all about collaboration and openness. We're talking GIT, baby! This means anyone can join the party, contribute their ideas, and help shape the future of our documentation.

But wait, there's more! Our Confluence site is also home to an incredible wealth of knowledge - take a look at [https://interfaceware.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CSHC/pages/2894954498/IguanaX](https://interfaceware.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CSHC/pages/2894954498/IguanaX) to see what's possible.

Now, we've got a dream team member who wants to create some awesome scripts that can extract data from these systems and convert it into markdown. Can you imagine the possibilities? We're talking public interfaces, AI-powered tools, and scripting magic - all coming together to make our lives easier!

And, we get to share our love for open-source software with the world! Who says we're stuck on outdated platforms like Confluence and WordPress? Let's create something beautiful and release it into the wild.
