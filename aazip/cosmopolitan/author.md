# Justine Tunney

Is a well-known software engineer and open source contributor who started the Cosmopolitan project around 2020. She has an interesting background as a former Google engineer and is also known for work on other ambitious projects like Redbean (a portable web server) and early involvement in open source and activism. Cosmopolitan is her brainchild, and she remains its principal author and maintainer.

She's another neurodivergent.

She is a transwoman.
