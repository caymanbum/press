# Cloverleaf Partnership  

Cloverleaf, one of the originators in the integration engine market, is an ideal partner for this initiative.

**How this partnership could work:**

We build and deliver the “gulp” utility, which might function like this:

```shell
> gulp.com  --find cloverleaf --password jJdSkjwkj23jk45
Finding....
Located: C:\\\\PROGRAM FILES\\\\INFOR CLOVERLEAF\\\\
Securing....
Secured:  Package C:\\\\PROGRAM FILES\\\\CLOVERLEAF.com
>
```

**Core Mechanism:**

After installation, the “gulp” utility locks down Cloverleaf instances, allowing only maintained, up-to-date systems to remain in operation. Infor can then securely roll out this utility to all its customers, including legacy environments where customers may have allowed their maintenance to lapse.

**Topline Revenue Growth:**  
With “gulp” enforcing regular software updates and maintenance, customers are compelled to stay current on their maintenance contracts. This results directly in increased recurring revenue:  
- **Expired maintenance contracts are now renewed** so clients can keep their Cloverleaf systems running.  
- **Unmaintained instances are brought under active support**, expanding the addressable customer base.  
- **Legacy users are incentivized to modernize**, driving more predictable and reliable cash flow.

**Operational Cost (Bottom Line) Reductions:**  
At the same time, the utility enables Infor to automate and dramatically simplify customer management:
- **Automated software compliance** reduces time and cost spent chasing overdue accounts or defending against security incidents from unpatched systems.  
- **Streamlined billing**: If maintenance lapses, access to the software can be paused until reinstatement—cutting down on collections, exceptions, and manual intervention.
- **Reduced liability** from exposed, unsupported installations further lowers risk and downstream costs.

**Result: Higher Profitability**  
By using the "gulp" utility to both secure the software and enforce ongoing maintenance, Infor increases top line revenue **and** reduces back-office costs. The delta—operating profit—increases significantly.

**In Short:**  
The introduction of “gulp” not only makes Cloverleaf safer and more robust for everyone, but directly fuels growth in revenue while pushing down operational costs—combining both levers to drive a step-change in profitability for the business unit.

**Additional Outcome:**  
Once all legitimate users are upgraded, “gulp” can be deployed more broadly to retire or isolate any lingering, unsecured installs—further safeguarding the ecosystem and setting a new standard for software responsibility in healthcare integration.

We're still early in the planning stages and open to a variety of commercial models to fund this investment.
