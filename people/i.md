# People

iNTERFACEWARE’s new form isn’t that of a traditional company with shareholders, stocks, and employees. Instead, it is primarily myself, Eliot Muir, working alongside a network of people and companies. I refer to this as an affiliate model, and it has proven to be remarkably effective.

Through this arrangement, I’m able to collaborate with groups of highly competent individuals. This flexible structure allows us to develop products and code with a level of competence and speed that far surpasses what is possible in a conventional corporate environment. It’s truly an amazing way to work.

Looking back, I wish I had discovered and adopted this model sooner. However, I’m grateful to have found it now—even if it’s later than I would have liked.

We live and learn and what doesn't kill us makes us stronger.
