# Plan

This plan was developed through a collaborative process involving extensive consultation with all major stakeholders. Rather than being the result of isolated decision-making, it reflects the diverse perspectives and expertise of those who will be integral to its implementation and success. By engaging stakeholders throughout the planning process, we have ensured that the plan is both comprehensive and grounded in the practical realities faced by our community and organization.

Iguana 6, although deeply loved by its user base for its stability, ease of use, and performance, is based on a very old codebase. There is only one person in the world who truly understands this codebase in depth: Eliot Muir. Eliot Muir, the founder and chief architect, is uniquely qualified and trusted to work with this codebase. His expertise is essential to ensure that the delicate process of modernizing Iguana 6 is handled in a way that maintains backward compatibility.
