# How Affiliates Work with Eliot

Eliot highly values transparent and honest relationships with his affiliates. His approach is based on integrity and open communication to ensure success for all parties involved.

He believes that transparency with clients is essential, especially regarding pricing. Clients should always have a clear understanding of any markups for services such as ECG questionnaires, compliance paperwork, and other requirements. This openness fosters trust and long-term collaboration.

To facilitate a seamless and reliable process, Eliot typically requests read-only access to affiliate intermediary bank accounts established to receive payments. This measure is intended solely to promote transparency and ensure all transactions are handled efficiently.

We understand that affiliates play a significant role in managing the challenges clients may present. Eliot is committed to fair compensation and is comfortable with arrangements where affiliates receive the majority share, particularly when they are responsible for most of the client relationship management.

Loyalty from our affiliates is highly valued. We believe in the exceptional quality of the Iguana product and expect our affiliates to share this commitment. If affiliates consistently prioritize competing products, Eliot may reconsider their affiliate status. We encourage clients seeking a strong partnership with the source of value—Eliot—to work with like-minded affiliates who are aligned with our standards and priorities.
