# SOC2 like many security standards is all about compliance

This matters.  And it we should consider applying to broader
aspects of our lives.

## SOC 2 Control GWH-001: Public Washroom Anonymous Interaction Procedure

**Purpose:** To ensure all participants maintain compliance, privacy, and operational consistency during voluntary after-hours engagement.

- Verify that all parties are consenting adults.
- Do not collect personally identifiable information.
- Maintain strict segregation of duties.
- No verbal disclosure of identity without prior authorization.
- Log nothing. Audit nothing. **Plausible deniability** is the **retention policy**.
- If anyone asks why this procedure exists, direct them to the **Risk Committee**.
