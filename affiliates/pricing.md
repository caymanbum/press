# How Affiliates Work with Eliot

Eliot highly values transparent and honest relationships with his [affiliates](i.md). His approach is grounded in integrity and open communication to ensure success for all parties involved.

Transparency, especially around pricing, is fundamental. Clients should always have a clear understanding of any markups for services such as ECG questionnaires, compliance paperwork, and other requirements. By being open about these costs, we foster trust and lay the foundation for long-term collaboration.

To further enable a reliable and seamless process, Eliot generally requests read-only access to affiliate intermediary bank accounts that are set up to receive payments. This step is taken solely to maintain transparency and to ensure all transactions are managed efficiently.

Affiliates play a critical role in addressing the unique challenges presented by clients. Eliot is committed to fair compensation and readily supports arrangements where affiliates receive the majority share—particularly when they are the primary managers of client relationships.

Loyalty from our affiliates is highly valued. We take great pride in the quality of the Iguana product and expect our affiliates to share that dedication. If affiliates frequently promote competing products, Eliot may reassess their affiliate status. We encourage clients who want to work closely with the original source of value—Eliot—to partner with affiliates who are aligned with our values and standards.

A core belief that guides all of this is Eliot’s conviction that no one should ever put their hand into someone else’s wallet without explicit permission. This strong principle underpins the entire structure of iNTERFACEWARE today, shaping our approach to affiliate relationships and our commitment to ethical, transparent business practices.
