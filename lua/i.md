# Lua is a small simple secure dynamic language

**Lua** is a small, simple, and secure dynamic programming language. Known for its fast performance, ease of integration, and lightweight footprint, Lua is widely used for scripting in games, embedded systems, and applications where efficiency is crucial. Its straightforward syntax and dynamic nature make it accessible for beginners while powerful enough for advanced users.

It's the only sane choice in a world where security is going to be tantamont.

Fast, simple and secure.  What is not to love?
