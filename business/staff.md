# If you miss former iNTERFACEWARE staff 

For customers who may find themselves pining for certain former iNTERFACEWARE staff, Eliot would like to recognize that, yes, some of those individuals were indeed quite pleasant and enjoyable to work with. Many could adapt their communication styles with surprising agility and, dare we say, were genuinely nice to be around. In fact, their ability to carry on amiable and persuasive conversations—so polished and engaging—could almost make you forget how often the underlying technical details were, well, a little vague.

If you truly find yourself missing that environment, you are enthusiastically encouraged to bring those rays of sunshine directly onto your own team. After all, what better way to brighten up your meetings and email exchanges?

We would, however, recommend exercising careful due diligence before acting on any technical advice or code suggestions offered. In our experience, beneath the confident and friendly exterior, there were occasional gaps when it came to producing reliable, robust, and well-tested systems. So please, enjoy their company—but double-check their work, just to be safe.
